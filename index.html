Cre 8

IT'S ALL ABOUT...

Cre8 is an inter-school event hosted by Lecole Chempaka, in which over 30 schools are invited to participate. The interaction of students carves out an innovative union of 15+ events. Our highlights are FusionX, Encore, Project Runway,The Crown, etc. Although Cre8 is being hosted through an online platform for the first time, we do not let it get in the way of the Cre8 spirit. So, here we are with new events such as Gaming, Digital Art, Animation, etc.

About

WHO WE ARE...

Lecole Chempaka, “Silver Rocks” is a premier co-educational Senior Secondary Educational institution situated at Edavacode, Sreekariyam, Thiruvananthapuram. The school was founded in 1998 and is affiliated to the Council for Indian School Certificate Examinations. The teaching method is pivoted around “The Chempaka Way " and a dynamic atmosphere for the students. Lecole Chempaka’s compassion for other people has given birth to ‘Urjja’; a charitable organisation driven by the students.

About Us

Lecole Chempaka, “Silver Rocks” is a premier co-educational Senior Secondary Educational institution situated at Edavacode, Sreekariyam, Thiruvananthapuram. The school was started in 1999 and is affiliated to the Council for Indian School Certificate Examinations vide KE100 and offers Science, Commerce and Humanities stream in ISC. The results of ICSE and ISC have been outstanding over the years.
Lecole Chempaka was founded in 2011 by Mr. Vernon Gomez and Dr. Daphne Gomez, L’école Chempaka International has become the household name for quality international education. Since its inception, it has been Indian in Ethos, Global in outlook with the holistic aims to encourage International understanding and to support modern curriculum development, to set standards which are recognized worldwide by encouraging good and innovative modes of teaching and to enable the students to become confident and well informed in a modern technological world.

The teaching method is pivoted around “The Chempaka Way '' that offers a stress-free, student-friendly and dynamic atmosphere where learning happens naturally, creatively and meaningfully.
The L’école Chempaka Society for Educare also manages L’école Chempaka ‘Serene Valley’, which will be affiliated to CISCE, L’école Chempaka International which follows the Cambridge Curriculum, L’école Chempaka ‘Horizon’, a primary school at Vellayambalam and L’école Chempaka ‘Silver Oak’, a primary CBSE Curriculum school.

Lecole Chempaka’s compassion for other people has given birth to ‘Urjja’; a charitable organisation driven by the students.
